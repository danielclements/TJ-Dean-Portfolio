Hi, my name is Tj Dean

I’m a apprentice tattoo artist living in Orihuela Costa, Spain.My current mentor is Adam Dutton.


My passion for tattoos was cultivated at a young age, both my parents are tattoo artist , they are the main motivating factor behind my passion.
From spending countless hours in the Studio from a young age to being taught the art of sketching by my mum.
