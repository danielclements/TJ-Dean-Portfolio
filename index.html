Hi, my name is Tj Dean

I’m an apprentice tattoo artist living in Orihuela Costa, Spain. My current mentor is Adam Dutton.


My passion for tattoos was cultivated at a young age, both my parents are tattoos artist , they are the main motivating factor behind my passion.
From spending countless hours in the Studio from a young age to being taught the art of sketching by my mum.


My mentor is World Class Tattoo Artist Adam Dutton over the space of the last 2 years he has been teaching me everything he knows.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Does it Hurt ?

Yes, yes it does, but it's sooo worth it.

How much is a tattoo going to cost?

Depends what you want, how big, and how much detail is included.

How long will it take?

No comment.

What got you into tattooing?

Just a feeling that I got one day, I always loved art but portrayed it into different hobbies, ie. photography, video editing.

Why did it take you until now to start tattooing?

I just wasn't ready. All of a sudden I got asked if I'd like to try tattooing and it just hit me that this is what I want to do.

Do you work with/for your parents?

No I do not, I'm doing my own thing.

How long have you been tattooing?

I started in April 2018.

How did you learn to tattoo?

I'm the last apprentice of Adam 'Starfish' Dutton.
